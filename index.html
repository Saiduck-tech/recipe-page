Simple Omelette Recipe

an easy quick dish,perfect for any meal.this classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meats.


Preparation Time
  • Total:Approximately 10 mins

  • Preparation:5 minutes

  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs

  • Salt, to taste

  • Pepper, to taste

  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil

  • Optional fillings:cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats and herbs
